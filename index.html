Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Soul Arc is built on a simple principle: your inner world is yours. We collect as little as possible and keep what we do collect on your device.

What We Collect

Soul Arc stores all of your data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Your display name (entered during onboarding)
  • Your archetype result and onboarding responses
  • Journal entries, check-ins, and trigger logs
  • XP, level, streak, and progress data
  • Quest completion history

None of this data is transmitted to our servers. We do not have access to your journal entries or personal reflections.

Subscription Data

Soul Arc uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect your Apple ID, device identifiers, and purchase history to process and verify your subscription. RevenueCat's privacy policy governs that data. We do not receive or store your payment information.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not use analytics SDKs or tracking tools
  • We do not collect crash reports automatically
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use advertising networks or track you across other apps

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all Soul Arc data at any time by deleting the app from your iPhone. This permanently removes all journal entries, progress, and profile data.

Children's Privacy

Soul Arc is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Soul Arc after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Soul Arc → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Soul Arc → tap the paywall → tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase.

Where is my data stored?

All your journal entries, archetype results, and progress are stored locally on your iPhone. We never see or access your personal reflections.

Can I transfer my data to a new phone?

Yes — if you restore your new iPhone from an iCloud or local backup, Soul Arc data transfers automatically. Fresh installs will start from scratch.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Email us at support@soularc.app with a description of what happened, your iPhone model, and iOS version. Screenshots help a lot.

The app isn't working after an update.

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If that doesn't help, delete and reinstall — your subscription will restore and your local data may be recoverable from a backup. Email us if you need help.
