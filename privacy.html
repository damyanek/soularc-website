Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

Soul Arc stores your data — including your name, journal entries, archetype responses, and progress — locally on your device. We do not require account creation or login, and we do not collect your email address.

AI Feature: Speak to Umbra

When you use the Speak to Umbra feature, your messages are sent to our server (Supabase Edge Function) and forwarded to the Anthropic Claude API to generate responses. These messages are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers. Anthropic's data usage policy applies to this processing.

Third-Party Services

Soul Arc uses the following third-party services, each operating under their own privacy policies:

RevenueCat — manages subscription state and billing. They receive your Apple ID transaction data necessary to verify purchases. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store — handles all in-app purchases and subscription billing directly.

AppsFlyer — measures how new users discover Soul Arc so we can understand the effectiveness of our marketing. AppsFlyer may collect your device identifier (including Apple's IDFA, only if you grant permission when prompted), app launches, and subscription events. This data is used solely for marketing attribution. AppsFlyer Privacy Policy.

TikTok For Business — AppsFlyer may share aggregated attribution data with TikTok (operated by Bytedance Ltd.) solely to measure the performance of our TikTok ad campaigns. TikTok does not receive your personal journal content or in-app messages. TikTok Privacy Policy.

Advertising Measurement & Tracking

Soul Arc uses AppsFlyer and TikTok For Business to measure which marketing sources introduce new users to the app. When you first open Soul Arc, iOS will ask whether you allow us to track your activity across other apps and websites. This permission controls access to Apple's Advertising Identifier (IDFA).

If you choose "Ask App Not to Track", we do not receive the IDFA. Attribution will still occur using Apple's privacy-preserving SKAdNetwork framework, which provides only aggregated, anonymized campaign data — no personal identifiers.

You can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. We never sell your personal information to any third party — ever.

Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers, except for Umbra chat messages as described above. You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us at help@soularc.app.
